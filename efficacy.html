does one person matter?

impact

One person is not the entire solution, but movements are made of people changing norms, refusing participation, organizing, educating, and creating pressure. Individual action and collective action are not opposites.

better question

strategy

Instead of asking whether one person can fix the whole system, ask whether it makes sense to keep funding a harm once you understand it and have alternatives.
