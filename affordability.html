not a luxury diet

access

Veganism becomes expensive when it is built around specialty substitutes. It can be much cheaper when it is built around ordinary staples: beans, lentils, rice, pasta, oats, potatoes, tofu, frozen vegetables, peanut butter, and seasonal produce.

useful framing

class

Food access matters. It should be taken seriously without pretending that every animal product is necessary or that plant-based staples are automatically elitist.
