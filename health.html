the practical version

health

Vegan eating can be simple, but it should not be random. Build meals around starches, legumes, vegetables, fruit, nuts or seeds, and fortified foods where useful.

The main supplement to understand is B12. Many vegans also pay attention to vitamin D, iodine, calcium, iron, omega-3s, and protein depending on their diet and needs.

easy staples

pantry
  • beans, lentils, chickpeas, tofu, tempeh, and soy milk
  • rice, oats, potatoes, pasta, tortillas, and bread
  • frozen vegetables, greens, fruit, nuts, seeds, and peanut butter
  • fortified plant milks, nutritional yeast, and a reliable B12 source
